"...Melissa seeks to tell important stories with laughter and heart, whether it be by acting, writing, directing, or composing..."

Melissa found acting at a young age, and along the way has had the good fortune to work and learn alongside Broadway staples and regional titans. Inspired by her multi-hyphenate mentors, Melissa seeks to tell important stories with laughter and heart, whether it be by acting, writing, directing, or composing - whatever the specific project requires. She believes collaboration and specificity are key, bringing both to every room she's in.
Her most recent projects include To Be Here (Lead - Short Film), and Brecht's Galileo (Curator Priuli).
